Marketplace
===========

01. Take photos of the item
02. Get item details:
	a. Title
	b. Main description:
		* Condition
		* History
	c. Price:
		* Compare price I paid
		* Compair price they go for
	d. Collection/delivery details/restrictions
03. Decide ideal listing time
	a. Consider holidays
	b. Busy periods
	c. General availability
04. Add details in to Marketplace
